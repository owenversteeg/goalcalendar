Keep track of your goals

GoalCalendar helps you track your daily goals. After entering your daily goals, you can mark days on the calendar that you have accomplished your goals.

GoalCalendar can then give you statistics about your goals - including what percent of the time you accomplish a goal, when you accomplish your goals most, and how long you spend working toward a goal.
